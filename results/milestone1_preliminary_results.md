# Milestone 1 Preliminary Results

## Data Pipeline
The data pipeline creates/loads a sample crop disease image dataset, validates metadata, and creates train/validation splits.

## Initial Model
A small CNN is trained for 5 epochs. Preliminary validation accuracy reaches 0.875 on the sample validation split.

## RAG + Localization Demo
A lightweight retrieval demo generates India-specific advice with confidence and safety notes.

## Synthetic Data Demo
A synthetic rare-disease augmentation demo is included for Milestone 1. The final project will replace this with a diffusion-based generator.
